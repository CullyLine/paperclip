## Roster Overview

50 species across the first 3 zones, organized by zone and rarity. Each entry includes game name, real-world inspiration, rarity, habitat, spawn conditions, and visual description.

**Rarity distribution** (per M0 spec zone ranges):
- **Sunny Shore** (Common — Uncommon): 20 species (12 Common, 8 Uncommon)
- **Rocky Reef** (Common — Rare): 17 species (5 Common, 7 Uncommon, 5 Rare)
- **Kelp Forest** (Uncommon — Epic): 13 species (4 Uncommon, 5 Rare, 4 Epic)

**Total**: 50 species | 17 Common | 19 Uncommon | 10 Rare | 4 Epic

## Chain Requirement Details

### Prismatic Seahorse (Rare, Rocky Reef)
**Requires in aquarium**: Coral Crab (Uncommon, Rocky Reef) + Kelp Snail (Common, Rocky Reef)  
**Design intent**: Introduces chain mechanic early. Both prerequisite creatures are accessible in the same zone, making discovery achievable but requiring deliberate aquarium-building.

### Sunflower Star (Epic, Kelp Forest)
**Requires in aquarium**: Any 3 different sea star species (e.g., Sunny Star + Spiny Urchin + any other)  
**Design intent**: Rewards players who collected broadly rather than selling everything. Sea stars appear across zones, encouraging cross-zone play.

## Design Notes

1. **Every creature has personality.** Visual descriptions emphasize behavior and expression, not just appearance. Roblox players connect with creatures that feel alive — idle animations, reactions to the player, and distinct movement patterns are critical.

2. **Real biology grounds the fantasy.** Every creature maps to a real species, giving the art team clear reference material and giving the game educational credibility. Parents appreciate this.

3. **Condition-gated creatures create community.** Night-only, storm-only, and chain creatures will generate organic social media content ("HAS ANYONE FOUND THE KELP FOREST PHANTOM?") and wiki/guide culture.

4. **Chain requirements teach aquarium strategy.** The Prismatic Seahorse chain is simple (2 creatures, same zone) to introduce the mechanic. The Sunflower Star chain (3 sea stars across zones) rewards broad collection. Later zones should escalate complexity.

5. **Seasonal creatures are scarcity engines.** The Opalescent Squid appears only during Spring Bloom, creating urgency and long-term trade value. Each season should add 5-10 similar species.

6. **Rarity matches real ecological patterns.** Common creatures are abundant, small, and widespread (crabs, snails, barnacles). Epic creatures are large, rare, and ecologically significant (giant octopus, electric ray). This feels natural.

7. **Zone progression rewards exploration.** Sunny Shore is cute and approachable. Rocky Reef introduces "cool" predators (moray, mantis shrimp). Kelp Forest delivers awe (giant octopus, leopard shark). Each zone should feel like a meaningful upgrade in wonder.
