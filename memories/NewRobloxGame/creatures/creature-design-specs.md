## Design Philosophy

**Art Style**: Slime Rancher meets Finding Nemo — cute, colorful, low-poly stylized 3D with chibi proportions and oversized expressive eyes. Every creature should be instantly recognizable from its silhouette alone.

**Visual Hierarchy**:
- **Common** (7 creatures): Simple silhouettes, 2-3 color palette, maximum charm with minimal complexity. These are the workhorse collection pieces.
- **Uncommon** (3 creatures): Slightly more elaborate color palettes and surface detail. More visual personality and one standout visual feature each.

**Scale Reference**: Player character is approximately 5 studs tall in Roblox. Sizes below are relative to the player character.

## Visual Consistency Notes

### Shared Design Language
- All creatures have oversized eyes with catchlight highlights (two white dots)
- Rounded, soft geometry — no sharp edges or threatening features
- Subtle idle animations even when "still" (breathing, blinking, minor sway)
- Discovery animations should feel rewarding: splash particle, name card pop-up, brief sparkle

### Color Palette Constraints
- Common creatures: 2-3 main colors, muted/pastel tones
- Uncommon creatures: 3-4 main colors, one accent color that "pops" (the blue on Copper Blenny, the glass effect on Glassback Hermit, the flowing green on Ribbonweed Eel)
