## 1. Season 1 Pass Structure

### Overview

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Season Name** | Season 1: Midnight Velocity |
| **Duration** | 35 days |
| **Pass Price** | 749 Robux (one-time purchase per season) |
| **Total Tiers** | 50 |
| **Tracks** | Free (all players) + Premium (pass holders) |
| **Free Track Value** | ~30% of total reward value |
| **Total XP Required** | 15,000 Season XP |
| **Dedicated Player Completion** | ~25 days (2+ hours/day, all quests) |
| **Casual Player Completion** | ~35 days (1 hour/day, most quests) |

### XP Requirements Per Tier

Tiers start easy to give immediate gratification, then ramp. Early tiers unlock in a single play session; late tiers require sustained daily play.

| Tier Range | XP Per Tier | Cumulative XP | Design Intent |
|------------|-------------|---------------|---------------|
| 1–10 | 150 | 1,500 | Fast unlock — hook the player, show value immediately |
| 11–20 | 200 | 3,500 | Steady climb — player is committed, daily quests feel impactful |
| 21–30 | 300 | 6,500 | Mid-season ramp — weekly quests become essential |
| 31–40 | 400 | 10,500 | Push zone — dedicated players pull ahead of casual pace |
| 41–50 | 450 | 15,000 | Final stretch — completion feels like an achievement |

### XP Earning Sources

| Source | XP Per Occurrence | Frequency | Daily Avg (Dedicated) | Daily Avg (Casual) |
|--------|-------------------|-----------|----------------------|-------------------|
| Daily Quests (3/day) | 50 each | Daily | 150 | 100 (2 of 3) |
| Weekly Quests (3/week) | 200 each | Weekly | ~86 | ~86 |
| Milestone Quests (10 total) | 250 each | One-time | ~100 avg | ~71 avg |
| Distance XP | 1 per 500 studs | Per run | ~270 (2h play) | ~120 (1h play) |
| **Daily Total** | — | — | **~606** | **~377** |
| **Season Total (projected)** | — | — | **15,150 (25d)** | **13,195 (35d)** |

Casual players who miss a few daily quests hit exactly 15,000 over 35 days with consistent play. Players who fall behind can purchase tier skips (see 1.4).

### Tier Skip Monetization

| Product | Price | Effect |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Skip 1 Tier | 75 Robux | Instantly unlock current tier rewards |
| Skip 5 Tiers | 325 Robux (13% discount) | Unlock next 5 tiers |
| Skip 10 Tiers | 599 Robux (20% discount) | Unlock next 10 tiers |

Tier skips are positioned as "catch up" for players who buy the pass late in the season. This is where Grow a Garden generates significant secondary revenue beyond the 749R pass price.

### Free Track vs Premium Track Reward Distribution

The free track gives enough rewards to keep non-paying players engaged and to demonstrate the value proposition of the premium track. Every free-track tier has a visible "locked" premium reward beside it, creating constant upgrade pressure.

| Reward Category | Free Track Share | Premium Track Share |
|----------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Coins | 40% of total coin rewards | 100% of total coin rewards |
| Gems | 25% of total gem rewards | 100% of total gem rewards |
| Crystals | 0% (premium-exclusive) | 100% of crystal rewards |
| Exclusive Pets | 1 (Epic tier) | 2 (Epic + Legendary) |
| Exclusive Car | 0 | 1 (Tier 50 reward) |
| Cosmetics | 1 trail | 3 trails + 1 name tag + 1 chat tag |
| Consumables | 30% of total boosts | 100% of total boosts |

## 2. Season 1 Theme & Rewards

### Theme: Midnight Velocity

**Visual Direction:** Dark highways under a starlit sky. Deep purple-to-black gradients with electric blue and white accents. Headlights cut through fog. Neon mile markers glow on the roadside. Think midnight street racing meets cyberpunk, but grounded in the DAC art style.

**Why This Theme Works:**
- Visually distinct from all 4 existing worlds (none are night-themed)
- "Midnight" and "velocity" are aspirational words that resonate with the car/speed fantasy
- Dark backgrounds make neon reward items pop visually — seasonal rewards look incredible in screenshots
- Night-racing aesthetic is universally cool to the Roblox 8-14 demographic

## 3. Season 1 Limited Egg: Midnight Egg

### Overview

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **ID** | `midnight_egg` |
| **Name** | Midnight Egg |
| **Price** | 1,500 Gems + 50 Midnight Tokens |
| **Availability** | Season 1 only (35 days) — permanently removed after |
| **Location** | Special seasonal egg pedestal in lobby |
| **Visual** | Black egg with swirling purple galaxies and glowing blue cracks |

### Midnight Tokens

A temporary 5th currency exclusive to Season 1. Creates a dedicated earning loop that keeps players engaged with seasonal content beyond the battle pass XP system.

| Earning Method | Tokens Per Occurrence |
|----------------|----------------------|
| Daily Quest Completion (all 3) | 10 |
| Weekly Quest Completion (all 3) | 30 |
| Every 25,000 studs driven during the season | 5 |
| Milestone Quest Completion | 15 each |
| Every rebirth during the season | 3 |

**Earning Rate:** Active players earn ~25-40 tokens/day. One Midnight Egg costs 50 tokens (~2 days of dedicated play) plus 1,500 gems. This pacing lets dedicated players hatch ~12-15 Midnight Eggs during the season.

## 4. Seasonal Event Structure

### 4.1 World Integration

#### Lobby Transformation: Midnight Lobby

- **Skybox:** Night sky with visible stars, a large crescent moon, and subtle aurora effects
- **Lighting:** Dramatically reduced ambient light; all interactive zones gain neon-blue glow-up outlines
- **Decorations:** Glowing lamp posts along pathways, floating lanterns, fog at ground level
- **Music:** Chill synthwave/lo-fi driving soundtrack — relaxed but atmospheric
- **Seasonal Pedestal:** New egg station for the Midnight Egg with swirling galaxy particle effects

#### Highway Transformation: Midnight Highway

All 4 worlds get a visual overlay during the season (no gameplay changes, purely cosmetic):

| World | Midnight Overlay |
|-------|-----------------|
| Grasslands | Night sky, firefly particles along the roadside, moon visible |
| Desert | Starlit sky, cool-toned sand, distant aurora on the horizon |
| Frozen Tundra | Northern lights intensified, bioluminescent ice patches on road edges |
| Neon City | Enhanced neon glow, rain effect on road surface, reflections |

These overlays cost no additional art budget — they're lighting and particle changes only. But they make the entire game feel "new" for returning players, which is critical for the first-session re-engagement hook.

### 4.2 Daily Rotating Challenges

In addition to the standard daily quests (which give Season XP), the season adds **Midnight Challenges** — a single rotating challenge per day that rewards Midnight Tokens and bonus items.

| Day Pattern | Challenge | Reward |
|-------------|-----------|--------|
| Monday | **Moonlit Marathon** — Drive 25,000 studs in one session | 15 Tokens + 5,000 Coins |
| Tuesday | **Egg Frenzy** — Hatch 5 eggs | 10 Tokens + 2 Luck Boosts |
| Wednesday | **Rebirth Rush** — Rebirth 2 times | 12 Tokens + 10 Crystals |
| Thursday | **Speed Demon** — Complete a run at maximum speed stat | 10 Tokens + 2 Speed Boosts |
| Friday | **Night Owl** — Play for 30 minutes total | 15 Tokens + 100 Gems |
| Saturday | **Collector's Day** — Equip 6+ different pets | 10 Tokens + 5,000 Coins |
| Sunday | **Sunday Funday** — Complete any 2 Daily Quests | 20 Tokens + 10,000 Coins |

### 4.3 Community Milestone Goals

Server-wide goals that all players contribute to. Progress is tracked on a display board in the lobby. When the community hits the target, ALL players receive the reward (even those who didn't directly contribute — this drives social media sharing and "we did it" moments).

| Milestone | Target | Reward (All Players) | Expected Timeline |
|-----------|--------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Midnight Miles I** | 50,000,000 total studs driven by all players | 5,000 Coins + 25 Gems | Days 1-3 |
| **Midnight Miles II** | 500,000,000 total studs driven | 15,000 Coins + 50 Gems + 10 Tokens | Days 5-10 |
| **Midnight Miles III** | 5,000,000,000 total studs driven | 50,000 Coins + 100 Gems + 25 Tokens | Days 15-25 |
| **Hatch-a-Thon** | 1,000,000 total eggs hatched by all players | 20,000 Coins + 50 Gems + "Community Champion" title (temporary) | Days 10-20 |
| **Rebirth Revolution** | 100,000 total rebirths by all players | 25 Crystals + 30 Tokens + exclusive "Midnight Star" trail (permanent) | Days 20-35 |

**Design Notes:**
- Targets scale with expected player count (assuming 2,000-10,000 DAU)
- Early milestones are easy to create momentum and excitement
- The final milestone rewards a permanent-exclusive cosmetic ("Midnight Star" trail) that is ONLY available through this community goal — drives urgency and social media posts
- Display board updates in real-time in the lobby

### 4.4 End-of-Season Rewards

When the season ends, top performers receive bonus rewards based on leaderboard position during the season.

| Leaderboard | Top 1 | Top 10 | Top 100 | Top 1,000 |
|-------------|-------|--------|---------|-----------|
| **Most Season XP Earned** | Exclusive "Midnight Champion" title + 500 Gems + 100 Crystals | 300 Gems + 50 Crystals | 150 Gems + 25 Crystals | 50 Gems |
| **Most Distance Driven (Season)** | Exclusive "Speed King" title + 500 Gems + 100 Crystals | 300 Gems + 50 Crystals | 150 Gems + 25 Crystals | 50 Gems |
| **Most Midnight Eggs Hatched** | Exclusive "Egg Master" title + 500 Gems + 100 Crystals | 300 Gems + 50 Crystals | 150 Gems + 25 Crystals | 50 Gems |

Titles from end-of-season rewards are **permanent** — they become social proof that the player was there during Season 1. This is one of the strongest FOMO mechanics available because the value only increases over time as the game grows.
