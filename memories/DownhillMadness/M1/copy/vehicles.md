# Downhill Madness — M1 Vehicle Descriptions

## Speedster

**Display Name:** Speedster

**Tagline:** Built for one thing — getting there first.

**Full Description:**
The Speedster is pure velocity in a tin-foil shell. Blazing acceleration and razor-sharp handling make it the fastest ride on the hill, but one bad hit and you're scrap metal. Drive clean or don't drive at all.

**Flavor Text:**
_"Speed is a lifestyle. Brakes are a suggestion."_

---

## Tank

**Display Name:** Tank

**Tagline:** Nothing stops the Tank. Nothing.

**Full Description:**
Slow off the line but built like a fortress on wheels. The Tank shrugs off collisions that would total any other car and plows through obstacles like they're cardboard. You won't win on speed — you'll win because you're still rolling when everyone else is in pieces.

**Flavor Text:**
_"If the hill won't move, neither will I."_

---

## All-Rounder

**Display Name:** All-Rounder

**Tagline:** No surprises. No excuses.

**Full Description:**
Decent speed, decent armor, decent handling — the All-Rounder doesn't dominate in any stat, but it never lets you down either. The perfect pick if you want a ride that adapts to whatever the hill throws at you.

**Flavor Text:**
_"Jack of all trades. Master of not dying."_
