## Clip Acquisition Notes

- Most of these scenes are available on YouTube as individual clips uploaded by studios or fans
- Search using the provided keywords + "scene" or "clip" suffix
- Prefer official studio uploads (higher quality, less likely to be removed)
- For yt-dlp: download at 720p minimum for quality, trim with ffmpeg
- Build the local SQLite cache indexed by emotion tags for reuse across generations
